Production
We have a wide range of packaging capabilities including bottles, canisters, tubs, tubes, refill pouches, and bulk quantities.

Our state-of-the-art production lines are a cornerstone of our commitment to excellence, boasting the capability to produce up to 50 bottles per minute. Our facilities, located in Switzerland and China, are ISO 13485 certified, reflecting our dedication to quality and safety in manufacturing. Our production process is a testament to technological advancement, being fully automated and encompassing stages from bottle erecting and leak checking to filling, capping, labeling, and even vision inspection. This end-to-end automation ensures precision and efficiency, culminating in advanced cartoning, palletizing, and pallet stretching processes.

In addition to our impressive production capabilities, we take pride in our innovative labeling solutions, offering booklet labels that can include up to 30 different languages, catering to a global market. Our product range includes high-quality disinfectant wipes, manufactured with the same attention to detail and quality assurance that characterizes all our products. This comprehensive approach to production, combined with our strategic locations in Switzerland and China, enables us to deliver superior disinfectant products that meet the rigorous demands of various industries worldwide, ensuring consistent quality and reliability for our customers.

Logistics
We store your products in our warehouse and organize your shipment.

In the realm of logistics, United Disinfectant Manufacturers AG excels by providing secure storage for your products within our advanced warehouse facilities and managing the intricacies of shipment organization. Our logistics team, including staff with IATA qualifications, ensures that whether your shipment travels by air, road, or sea, the process is seamless and efficient. With profound expertise in global shipping, we handle everything from national deliveries within Europe to international freight to distant locations like New Zealand. Our aim is to deliver your order of infection control products promptly, safely, and in a cost-conscious manner, underpinned by a readiness to assist with any logistic challenges that may arise, ensuring that your products are in capable hands from the moment they leave our production line until they reach your specified destination.

Regulatory support
We get your product CE market and register it with competent authorities around the world.

We ensure that your product achieves CE marking and is registered with the relevant authorities globally, adhering to stringent compliance standards. Manufactured disinfectants by us are distributed across more than 60 countries, and our specialized regulatory department vigilantly updates and verifies that our disinfectants, their labeling, and safety data sheets (SDS) fulfill the latest regulatory demands. We provide branded SDS that comply with REACH and GHS regulations in over 40 languages.

Navigating the complex landscape of global healthcare infection control, with its myriad local and national regulations and varied interpretations of international standards, is a challenge we are equipped to meet. Our in-house Regulatory and Quality Team is dedicated to maintaining ongoing compliance and the highest quality of all products manufactured. This dedication gives our customers the assurance they need to successfully register products with their national medical regulatory authorities, backed by expert advice and the necessary documentation to streamline this intricate process.
